About me

I'm a PhD student at the Chair of IT Security at KIT. I have a background in mathematics, especially graph theory and algebraic geometry. My current research interests lie in privacy-preserving data analytics with a strong focus on privacy-preserving trajectory data publishing with differential privacy guarantees.
