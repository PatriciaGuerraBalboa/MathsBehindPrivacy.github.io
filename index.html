About me

I am a PhD Student at the IT Security group of Karlsruhe Institut of Tecnology , in Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Thorsten Strufe . I started my undergraduate studies at the Universidad de Santiago de Compostela, where I then did my master and bachelor. <\p>

Research interests

I study primarily C*-algebras and inverse semigroup theory, covering from classification of C*-algebras to hypertraces and Følner sequences. Since the notion of amenability is so widespread, however, I am as well interested in other areas, such as coarse geometry, groupoids and K-theory.
